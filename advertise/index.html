FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before partnering with us

Can we sponsor a single event instead of a full package?

Yes! Our Silver package is designed for single-event sponsorship. You can also discuss custom one-time arrangements with our team for specific events.

What kind of brands do you work with?

We work with educational brands, coaching institutes, local businesses, FMCG brands, sports goods companies, and any brand that wants to reach students and families in J&K. We do review all partnerships to ensure brand alignment with our educational mission.

How many people will see my brand at a single event?

A typical Infoma event has 200–500 direct participants plus accompanying parents and teachers. With digital coverage, a single event reaches 2,000–5,000 people through our social channels and WhatsApp networks.

Do you provide a post-event report?

Absolutely. Gold and Platinum partners receive a detailed post-event report including photos, reach analytics, social media impressions, and attendance numbers.

Can I provide my own branding materials?

Yes. You can supply your own standees, banners, or merchandise. We can also coordinate with our design team to create event-specific branding materials if needed (additional charges may apply).
