Quiz Rules & Guidelines

Please read these rules before participating

1

Submit Only Once

Each student may submit the form only once. Multiple submissions are not allowed.

2

Enter Your School Name Correctly

Write your school name accurately — results and certificates are issued based on the name provided.

3

Respect the Time Limit

Keep track of the submission deadline. Late entries will not be accepted.

4

Fair play

No external help or cheating allowed. This is purely a test of your own knowledge.

5

Trust the Results

Results will be announced within 3–5 days. You will be notified via email or social media.

6

Provide a Valid Email

Enter a valid email address — your certificate and result will be sent to this email.
