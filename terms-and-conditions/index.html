2 Our Services

Infoma Academy provides the following services to schools and students across Jammu & Kashmir:

  • Organization of inter-school quiz competitions across J&K
  • Online quiz platform for remote participation and real-time scoring
  • Award ceremonies and certificate distribution for participants and winners
  • Teacher training and academic support programs
  • School partnership and registration services

We reserve the right to modify, suspend or discontinue any part of our services at any time with reasonable notice.

3 Eligibility

To participate in Infoma Academy quiz events, the following conditions must be met:

  • Participants must be enrolled students of a registered partner school
  • Students must be in Classes 6–12 to participate in the relevant division
  • Schools must be located within the Union Territory of Jammu & Kashmir or Ladakh
  • Registration must be completed by the school's authorized representative
  • Participants must comply with the age and class group assigned to their division

4 School Registration

Schools wishing to partner with Infoma Academy must provide accurate and complete information during registration. By submitting a registration form, the school representative confirms:

  • All information provided is accurate and up to date
  • They have the authority to register the school on its behalf
  • They agree to notify Infoma Academy of any changes to contact information
  • The school will cooperate in organizing participation logistics

5 Code of Conduct

All participants and school representatives are expected to maintain the highest standards of integrity and sportsmanship. The following are strictly prohibited:

  • Use of unfair means, cheating, or impersonation during quiz events
  • Disruptive, abusive or disrespectful behavior toward other participants or staff

  • Any attempt to manipulate scores or results
  • Sharing confidential quiz content before or after events

Violation of the code of conduct may result in immediate disqualification and/or ban from future events.
