Our Mission

What Drives Us Every Single Day

We exist to give every student in Jammu & Kashmir a world-class stage to think, compete, and shine — regardless of where they're from.

"To democratize academic competition in J&K by building the region's most inclusive, professional, and inspiring quiz platform — one that gives every student an equal shot at excellence."

— The Infoma Academy Mission Statement

01

Reach Every School

Our goal is to partner with every school in J&K — from government schools in rural areas to elite institutions in urban centers — ensuring no student is left out of the opportunity to compete.

02

Build Confident Thinkers

Beyond winning trophies, we aim to build students who are curious, articulate, and confident in their knowledge — skills that last a lifetime and open doors beyond school.

03

Elevate Education Standards

By bringing national-level competition formats to J&K, we raise the bar for what students and schools aspire to — directly improving the culture of learning in the region.

04

Empower Educators

Teachers are the backbone of every great student. We equip them with quiz resources, training, and frameworks to nurture a sustainable quizzing culture from within their schools.

HOW WE DO IT

The three pillars of our approach

Every program we design is built on these three foundations

Professional Standards

Every quiz we organize follows national competition formats — structured rounds, impartial judging, digital scoring, and grand award ceremonies. We take every event seriously so students feel the weight of real achievement.

Community Inclusion

We go beyond tier-1 cities. We actively partner with schools in smaller towns and rural areas, offering subsidized or free participation to ensure access is never a barrier to competition.

Measurable Impact

We track student progress, school engagement, and educator feedback after every event. Impact isn't a buzzword for us — it's a metric we measure and improve with each passing year.
