FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Infoma Academy, our quizzes, and how to participate.

General
What is Infoma Academy?
Infoma Academy is Jammu & Kashmir's first and only dedicated inter-school quiz platform. We organize professional, large-scale quiz competitions for schools across J&K — from Jammu to Srinagar to Leh — covering a wide range of academic subjects.
Is Infoma Academy the only quiz platform in J&K?
Yes! As of 2026, Infoma Academy remains the only organization in Jammu & Kashmir that specializes in professional inter-school quiz championships at scale. No other body organizes quizzes across the region with this level of structure, digital infrastructure, and school partnerships.
When was Infoma Academy founded?
Infoma Academy was founded in 2023 in Jammu, with the mission of creating a world-class academic competition platform for students across the J&K Union Territory.
Schools & Registration
How can my school register with Infoma Academy?
Simply click the "Register Your School" button on our homepage or contact us via the Contact page. Fill in your school's details and our team will reach out within 24 hours to discuss participation and upcoming quiz events.
Is there a fee for schools to join?
Participation fees vary by event type and size. Some introductory events are free for new partner schools. We also offer subsidized participation for government schools and schools from rural areas to ensure inclusive access. Contact us for pricing specific to your school.
Which boards/curricula do you support?
We support all major curricula including CBSE, JKBOSE, and ICSE. Our question sets are designed to be curriculum-agnostic at the core — emphasizing general knowledge, reasoning, and subject understanding rather than rote syllabus recall.
How many students can a school send per quiz?
This varies by event format. Typically, team quizzes allow 2–3 students per team, and schools can register multiple teams. Individual quizzes allow unlimited participants per school. Specific limits are communicated during event registration.
Quiz Format & Participation
What subjects do the quizzes cover?
Our quizzes cover General Knowledge, Science & Technology, History & Geography, Current Affairs, Sports, Literature, Math & Logic, and J&K-specific knowledge. Visit our Quiz Categories page for the full list of topics and upcoming events.
Are quizzes conducted online or offline?
We conduct both! Preliminary rounds are often online through our digital platform, while grand finale events are held in-person at partner venues across Jammu and Srinagar. Some regional events are fully offline to accommodate schools with limited internet access.
Do students receive certificates for participating?
Yes! Every participant receives a digital certificate of participation. Winners receive printed certificates, trophies, and medals at the award ceremony. Top performers in major events also receive cash prizes and scholarships from our sponsor partners.
For which age groups/grades are quizzes designed?
We have separate quiz tracks for: Junior (Classes 3–5), Middle (Classes 6–8), and Senior (Classes 9–12). Each track has age-appropriate question difficulty, format, and topics to ensure fair and meaningful competition.
Results & Awards
How are results announced?
Results are announced at the event's closing ceremony. Digital score sheets and rankings are shared with all registered schools via email within 48 hours. Results are also posted on our website and social media channels.
Can schools appeal quiz results?
Yes. We have a formal grievance and appeal process. Schools can raise objections within 48 hours of results publication. A review panel consisting of senior quiz experts examines the objection and announces a final decision within 5 working days.

Still have questions?

Our team is happy to help. Reach out and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
