Quiz Formats

Round Formats We Use

Our competitions feature multiple exciting round types

Buzzer Round

Fast-paced questions where the first team to buzz in gets to answer. Tests quick thinking and reflexes.

Visual Round

Images, logos, maps and photographs projected on screen. Identify what you see — geography, science, history.

Rapid Fire

30 questions in 60 seconds per team. Pure speed, breadth of knowledge and nerves of steel required.

Pass & Play

Teams can pass difficult questions to opponents. Strategy matters as much as knowledge in this format.

J&K Special Round

Questions exclusively about Jammu & Kashmir — history, culture, geography, personalities and current affairs.

Audio Round

Identify songs, speeches, sounds and audio clips. Tests a different dimension of knowledge and memory.

FAQ

Common Questions

Is there a registration fee?
No — registration for schools is completely free. Infoma Academy believes every student deserves access to quality competitions regardless of school budget.
Which classes/grades can participate?
We run separate competitions for Junior (Classes 6–8), Senior (Classes 9–10), and Senior Secondary (Classes 11–12) divisions so students compete within their age group.
How many students can a school send?
Each school can register up to 3 teams per division (one team = 2 to 4 students). There is no limit on observers/supporters who come to cheer.
Where are the events held?
Events are held at school auditoriums, convention centres and university halls across Jammu, Srinagar and Leh. Venue details are shared 2 weeks before the event.
Can private/convent schools participate?
Absolutely. We welcome government schools, private schools, convent schools, Kendriya Vidyalayas, Navodaya Vidyalayas and all CBSE/JKBOSE affiliated institutions.
