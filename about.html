About Infoma Academy

J&K's #1 School Quiz Platform

Our Mission

Infoma Academy is on a mission to revolutionize education in Jammu & Kashmir through engaging, competitive, and accessible quiz-based learning. We believe that learning should be fun, challenging, and rewarding.

What Makes Us Unique

We are proud to be the only platform in Jammu & Kashmir dedicated to organizing inter-school quizzes. Our platform bridges the gap between traditional learning and modern digital education.

Our Impact

With over 60,000+ active students and 150+ partner schools across J&K, Infoma Academy has become a trusted name in educational technology. Our students have shown remarkable improvement in competitive exam preparation.
