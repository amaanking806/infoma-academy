Who We Are

J&K's First & Only Quiz Platform

Infoma Academy was founded in 2020 with a singular vision — to bring structured, professional academic quizzing to the schools of Jammu & Kashmir. Before us, there was no dedicated platform doing this at scale in the region.

We organize inter-school quiz competitions that go beyond rote learning, encouraging students to think critically, compete fairly, and celebrate knowledge. From Jammu to Srinagar to Leh, we've reached over 50 schools and impacted 18,000+ students.

Our Mission

To make J&K's students the most quiz-competitive in India by building a culture of curiosity and knowledge.

Our Vision

A J&K where every school has an active quiz team and every student is proud to compete on the national stage.

Journey

Our Story So Far

From a single event to J&K's premier quiz platform

2020

Founded in Jammu

Infoma Academy started with our first inter-school quiz between 5 schools in Jammu city.

2021

Expanded to Kashmir Valley

Crossed the Banihal Pass — our quizzes reached Srinagar, Anantnag and Baramulla.

2022

Digital Platform Launch

Launched our online quiz portal enabling remote participation from schools across J&K.

2023

50 Schools Milestone

Crossed 50 partner schools and 10,000 students — a landmark for education in the region.

2024–25

Ladakh & National Ambitions

Expanded to Leh-Ladakh and are now preparing J&K students for national quiz championships.
