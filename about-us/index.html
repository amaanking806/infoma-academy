Founded 2023
OUR STORY

Born out of a gap in J&K's education

In 2023, we noticed that students in Jammu & Kashmir had exceptional academic talent but lacked a structured, professional platform to showcase it. National quiz competitions were out of reach, and local ones were few and informal.

Infoma Academy was founded to fill that void — to bring world-class quiz competitions right to the doorstep of J&K's schools, nurturing curiosity, confidence, and competitive spirit in every student.

  • First and only dedicated inter-school quiz platform in J&K
  • 50+ partner schools across Jammu, Kashmir & Ladakh
  • 15,000+ students impacted since inception
  • 120+ professionally organized quiz events

Our Core Values

Every decision we make is guided by these principles

We believe every question asked is a step toward brilliance. We design every quiz to spark genuine intellectual curiosity in students.

Inclusive Competition

Whether CBSE, JKBOSE or ICSE — every school, every student deserves equal access to quality competitive experiences.

Excellence in Execution

From question curation to ceremony — we hold every detail to national standards, ensuring students are proud to participate.

Rooted in J&K

We understand our region's unique educational landscape and design programs that resonate with the local culture and aspirations.

Fair scoring, unbiased question sets, and transparent results — trust is the foundation of every quiz we organize.

Long-term Growth

We measure success not by events held, but by students who grew more confident, curious, and capable of taking on the world.

OUR JOURNEY

Milestones that define us

From one quiz to a movement spanning all of J&K

2023

The Beginning

Infoma Academy was founded in Jammu with a bold mission — to create J&K's first professional inter-school quiz platform.

2024

First 10 Schools

We onboarded our first 10 partner schools and organized our debut inter-school quiz championship with over 300 participants.

2025

Going Digital

Launched our digital quiz platform enabling online rounds, real-time scoring, and remote participation across the region.

2025

Pan J&K Expansion

Expanded to the Kashmir Valley and Ladakh — now covering Jammu, Srinagar, Leh and surrounding districts.

2025–26

50+ Schools & 15K+ Students

Reached our biggest milestone — 50 partner schools, 15,000+ students impacted, and 120+ events organized successfully.
